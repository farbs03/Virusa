About

With all of the chaos currently ensuing in the US, it is important to be reminded of how vital taking the necessary health precautions such as wearing a mask and washing hands are in these times.

"

We may be done with the pandemic, but the pandemic is not done with us.

"

- Dr. Ashish Jha, director of the Harvard Global Health Institute

Virusa aims to hopefully give its users a good idea of the importance of said necessary precations through the simulations that it provides. The simulations can depict a situation where a group of people all wear masks, don't wear masks at all, etc. Although it won't be a 100% accurate depiction of reality, Virusa uses real probability and statistics to provide as accurate of a simulation as possible.

Through these simulations, Virusa should stand as a reminder that Coronavirus is still a very real thing in the US. If we stop being weary of it, it could spell a very bad future for the country.
